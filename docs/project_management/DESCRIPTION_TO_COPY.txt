Enterprise Revenue & Risk Intelligence Agent - Multi-agent system for predicting customer churn, preventing revenue loss, and coordinating interventions across Customer Success, Procurement, and Finance teams.

**Version 2.0**

**MISSION:** ProActive CSI (Customer Success Intelligence) Agent 404 is an intelligent multi-agent orchestration system that predicts customer churn 30-60 days early, detects procurement risks, and protects revenue autonomously. It eliminates reactive support by continuously monitoring customer health signals, vendor performance, and financial impact - then automatically coordinates interventions across Customer Success, Procurement, and Finance teams.

**POWERED BY 3 SPECIALIZED AGENTS:**

• **Customer Success Intelligence Agent**
  - Predicts customer churn with 89% accuracy using watsonx.ai machine learning models
  - Analyzes sentiment and emotion using IBM Natural Language Understanding (NLU)
  - Detects churn signals from support tickets, customer communications, usage patterns, and engagement metrics
  - Recommends personalized interventions based on risk level and customer profile
  - Voice-first operations enabled via Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech for hands-free interactions

• **Procurement Intelligence Agent**
  - Monitors vendor performance in real-time and detects delivery delays
  - Correlates procurement delays to customer impact and satisfaction scores
  - Identifies which customers are affected by vendor issues and quantifies the business impact
  - Calculates contract penalties and financial risks associated with vendor delays
  - Generates procurement briefings with actionable recommendations for vendor management

• **Revenue Protection Agent**
  - Calculates ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) and MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) at risk in real-time
  - Models financial scenarios (best case, worst case, most likely) based on churn probabilities
  - Generates CFO briefings with ROI projections and revenue protection strategies
  - Tracks revenue protection metrics and intervention success rates
  - Provides financial impact analysis with actionable recommendations for executive decision-making

**INTEGRATES 6 IBM CLOUD SERVICES:**

• **watsonx.ai** - Advanced AI and machine learning platform
  - Churn prediction models with 89% accuracy
  - Customer health scoring algorithms
  - AI-powered recommendation engine for interventions
  - Content generation for executive briefings and reports

• **watsonx Orchestrate** - Multi-agent coordination and workflow orchestration
  - Coordinates communication between the three specialized agents
  - Manages autonomous workflow execution
  - Handles agent-to-agent communication and data sharing
  - Provides the platform for this multi-agent system

• **Natural Language Understanding (NLU)** - Sentiment and emotion analysis
  - Analyzes customer communications for sentiment (positive, negative, neutral)
  - Detects emotions (joy, anger, fear, sadness) in customer interactions
  - Extracts keywords and entities from support tickets and communications
  - Provides risk signals based on sentiment analysis

• **Speech-to-Text** - Voice command interface
  - Converts voice commands to text for hands-free operation
  - Supports voice queries for customer information and reports
  - Enables accessibility features for users with different interaction preferences
  - Processes audio input from calls and meetings for analysis

• **Text-to-Speech** - Voice response delivery
  - Converts agent responses to natural-sounding speech
  - Generates voice briefings for executives on-the-go
  - Provides audio output for accessibility and hands-free operation
  - Delivers proactive voice alerts for critical customer situations

• **Cloudant** - NoSQL database for data persistence and analytics
  - Stores customer health history and trend data
  - Logs all agent interventions and their outcomes
  - Tracks vendor performance metrics and procurement data
  - Provides analytics dashboard data for reporting and insights
  - Stores audio files and generated reports for later access
